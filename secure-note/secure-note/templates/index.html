Secure Note

Customer safety comes first

We use advanced cryptography technology to protect our clients' notes.

SecNote is easy to use. First you need to create your own key. You can do this on the home page.

You can use different keys for different notes, or you can use one key for all.

To create a note you need to go to the create section and fill in the fields.

Be sure to attach your key, with it your notes will be securely encrypted.

It is very easy to view your notes.

You need to go to the check section and send your key.

Remember: you must keep your key in a safe place.
